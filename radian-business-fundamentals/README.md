# radian-business-fundamentals

The strategic layer ABOVE the funnel. Advisory-shaped, not DFY-automated. Typically sold to founders/CEOs, not Heads of Sales/Marketing.

## Why this plugin is shaped differently

Most plugins in this marketplace are operational — they execute workflows on data. This one is **Socratic / advisory**. Outputs are structured frameworks, audits, and POV documents — not automated runs. Used in workshop / off-site / CEO 1:1 contexts.
