# case-study-production

## When to use

- Customer hits an outcome milestone (TTV, ROI realization, expansion)
- Customer renews + grows (signal of full success)
- Customer publicly mentions the product positively
- Quarterly: target 5+ new case studies

## Core principle

**Case studies are the #1 most effective B2B content format** (CMI). 80%+ of buyers want a case study in their industry. Most companies produce <5/year when they should be producing 20-50+. The bottleneck: companies chase customers 6 months after the win, when memory has decayed and the customer has moved on.

## Workflow

1. **Detect a case-study-worthy moment**
   - Activation milestone hit
   - Metric moved (signal: customer mentions ROI on call or in email)
   - Renewal + expansion
   - Public positive mention (LinkedIn / press / community)

2. **Capture FRESH while enthusiasm is high**
   - CSM sends warm ask: "would you share your story?"
   - Customer commits or politely declines
   - If commit: 20-30 min interview within 14 days

3. **Interview structure (10 questions max)**
   - What was the problem before?
   - How were you solving it (or not)?
   - What changed when you started using us?
   - What's the concrete outcome / metric?
   - What surprised you?
   - Who else benefits (multi-stakeholder angle)?
   - What would you tell someone considering us?

4. **Produce in two formats**
   - **Quick win** (1 day turnaround): one-page PDF or web page — works for sales immediately
   - **Polished** (2-3 weeks): full case study with quotes / metrics / video / design

5. **Tag for searchability**
   - Industry / size / use case
   - Persona of champion
   - Outcome type (cost saved / revenue grown / time saved / risk reduced)
   - So sales / `reference-program-activation` can match to deals

## Output format

Per case study captured:

```markdown
*Case Study Draft — [Customer]*

*Tags:* [industry] / [size] / [use case] / [persona]
*Outcome metric:* [specific quantified win]

*Story (one-pager):*
**Before:** [customer's problem in their words]
**Change:** [what they did with the product]
**After:** [outcome with numbers]
**Quote:** "[verbatim from interview]"
**Champion:** [name, title] (logo + headshot rights confirmed: yes/no)

*Full case study production status:*
- Interview held: [date]
- Draft ready: [date]
- Legal review: [date]
- Customer sign-off: [date]
- Published: [date / url]
```

Quarterly production report:

```markdown
*Case Study Production — [Quarter]*

*New case studies published:* N (target 5+)
*Industries / segments covered:* [list]
*Use cases covered:* [list]
*Coverage gaps (target segments with 0 case studies):* [list]
*Sales utilization:* X case studies used in deals → Y deals closed-won
```

## Connectors

- CRM (customer + outcome data)
- CS tool (activation + milestone signals)
- Calendar (interview scheduling)
- Slack (CSM coordination)
- Content / CMS for publication

## Gotchas

- The 14-day rule: ask within 14 days of the win, do the interview within 14 days of the ask. Slower = colder.
- Legal + brand review is often the bottleneck — get the customer's legal involved early
- Quantify outcomes — vague "improved efficiency" is useless; "saved 12 hours/week per rep" sells
- Persona match matters MORE than logo size — a mid-market case study for a mid-market deal beats an enterprise logo
