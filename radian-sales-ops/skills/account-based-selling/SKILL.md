# account-based-selling

## When to use

- Top-tier named accounts (typically 50-200 per rep)
- Pre-launch into a new vertical / segment
- Quarterly named-account planning
- High-stakes deals needing all functions activated

## Core principle

**ABS-run accounts close at 2-3x the rate of standard outbound** (ITSMA / TOPO). 87% of B2B marketers running ABS see higher ROI. Most ABS programs underperform because sales runs accounts solo without orchestrated marketing/CS support.

## ABS motion components

1. **Account selection** — clear ICP criteria + strategic value
2. **Stakeholder mapping** — who's in the buying group, who's missing
3. **Per-account play plan** — multi-channel, multi-thread, sequenced
4. **Marketing air cover** — paid ads + content targeting the account
5. **Sales orchestration** — outbound, references, executive sponsorship
6. **Tracking + iteration** — account-level engagement, not lead-level

## Per-account play plan structure

| Phase | Activities |
|---|---|
| **Awareness** | LinkedIn ads, content distribution to known contacts, exec LinkedIn engagement |
| **Discovery** | Outbound to multiple stakeholders, signal monitoring, intent data |
| **Activation** | Meeting booked, custom demo, account-specific case study |
| **Validation** | Reference call, technical eval, exec-to-exec |
| **Close** | Procurement, legal, exec sponsor close |

## Workflow

1. Select target accounts (50-200 per rep)
2. Per account:
   - Stakeholder map (6-10 contacts identified, key roles)
   - Account brief (current state, intent signals, history, strategic value)
   - Custom play plan
   - Marketing targeting setup (LinkedIn audience, etc.)
3. Orchestrate across functions:
   - Marketing: ads + content
   - Sales: outbound + references + exec sponsor
   - SE: technical pre-sales
   - CS: existing customer expansion
4. Track engagement at the ACCOUNT level:
   - # contacts engaged
   - Touch frequency
   - Stage progression
5. Weekly account review with sales + marketing
6. Iterate per account — kill stagnant ones, double down on warming ones

## Connectors

- CRM (account + contact data)
- Marketing automation (campaign targeting)
- Ad platforms (LinkedIn especially)
- Intent providers (6sense / Bombora)
- LinkedIn Sales Nav
- Slack (cross-functional coordination)

## Gotchas

- ABS requires real cross-functional commitment — solo sales running ABS = standard outbound dressed up
- Don't try to run ABS on 500 accounts — 50-200 per rep max
- Track engagement at the ACCOUNT level, not lead level
- Kill stagnant accounts — orchestration isn't free
